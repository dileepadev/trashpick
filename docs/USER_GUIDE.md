## **Getting Started**

### **What is TrashPick?**

TrashPick is a platform that connects three types of users:

- **Trash Pickers** - People who have waste and want to sell it responsibly
- **Trash Collectors** - Entrepreneurs who buy waste from Pickers and sell to recycling centers
- **Recycling Centers** - Facilities that process and recycle materials

### **How to Register**

1. Open the TrashPick app
2. Tap the **"Sign Up"** button on the welcome screen
3. Enter your personal details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (choose a strong password)
   - Phone Number
   - Location
4. Select your user type:
   - **Trash Picker** - I want to sell my waste
   - **Trash Collector** - I want to buy and resell waste
5. Accept the terms and conditions
6. Verify your email address
7. Complete your profile with a photo and additional details

### **Signing In**

1. Open the TrashPick app
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Tap **"Sign In"**
4. You'll be directed to the home screen

## **Earning Points & Rewards**

### **Point System**

Earn points by completing activities on TrashPick:

| Activity | Points |
| ---------- | -------- |
| Posting a waste listing | 10 |
| Completing a sale | 50 |
| Buying waste | 30 |
| Writing a review | 15 |
| Referring a friend | 100 |

### **How to Redeem Rewards**

1. Open the **Settings** menu
2. Tap **"My Rewards"** or **"Points Dashboard"**
3. View your current points balance
4. Browse available rewards (discounts, badges, certificates)
5. Tap on a reward to redeem
6. Points will be deducted from your account

## **User Ratings & Reviews**

### **Leaving a Review**

After completing a transaction:

1. Go to **My Transactions**
2. Find the completed transaction
3. Tap **"Write Review"**
4. Rate the other user (1-5 stars)
5. Write your feedback (optional)
6. Submit

### **Why Reviews Matter**

- Build trust with other users
- Help the community identify reliable traders
- Improve seller/buyer reputation

## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

**Q: Is TrashPick free to use?**  
A: Yes! Registration and basic features are completely free. Earn points by using the app.

**Q: How safe is using TrashPick?**  
A: We encourage all transactions to happen in public places during daylight. Check user reviews before dealing with anyone new. Block suspicious users immediately.

**Q: Can I change my user type after registration?**  
A: Contact support through Settings > Help & Support to request a user type change.

**Q: What if I have a dispute with another user?**  
A: Go to Settings > Report Issue and describe the problem. Our team will review and help resolve it.

**Q: How are payments handled?**  
A: Payments are coordinated directly between users. We recommend using secure payment methods.

**Q: Can I cancel a listing?**  
A: Yes, tap the listing and select "Delete Listing." You can do this anytime.

**Q: How do I contact customer support?**  
A: Go to Settings > Help & Support or email <support@trashpick.app>
